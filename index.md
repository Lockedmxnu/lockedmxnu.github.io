## ğŸ› ï¸ Usage

To use MultiMeshPath3D, follow these steps:

1. Open your Godot project.
2. Add a **MultiMeshPath3D** node to your scene.
3. In the node properties, look for the **Mesh Variations** section.
4. Add your mesh resources here. These could be models of trees, fences, or any other object.
5. Adjust the transform properties to fit your vision:
   - **Scatter/Offset Radius**: Controls how far apart your instances will be.
   - **Rotation**: Set the angle of your objects as they follow the path.
   - **Scale**: Adjust the size of your instances.
